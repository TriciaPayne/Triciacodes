    • I wanted to share my story about becoming a Techmom! I was a stay at home mom for 11 years. I was married to a Tech-guy. We got divorced and it was hard to provide for myself. I tried a lot of different entry level jobs and nothing really was a great fit. I didn't feel valued and like I had any valuable gifts to contribute to the world. I found the link to the Techmoms website one day when I was scrolling on a Social Media app. I read the website and thought it would be so cool to have a supportive group of women to help me try to establish myself in a respectable career and to cheer me on all along the way. I got excited about it. Then the voices of doubt came into my mind and told me all of the reasons why it couldn't work out. Among them I heard messages like "you have to be there for your kids" and "you don't have what it takes to have a successful IT career". A lot of messages went through my mind and I was convinced that I would not be able to do it.
